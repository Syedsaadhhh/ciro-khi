**Speaker 1:** "Welcome to CIRO - Crisis Intelligence & Response Orchestrator. When disaster strikes Karachi, like the brutal Monsoon floods, our command centers are paralyzed by noise. Thousands of social media posts, conflicting weather data, and scattered traffic reports. Human operators simply cannot ingest this fast enough. 
Our solution? We removed the human bottleneck and replaced it with a 4-layer autonomous agent architecture built entirely on Google Gemini."

1. The Sifter classifies the Roman Urdu and pulls weather telemetry.
2. The Strategist calculates commuter impact and reroutes traffic.
3. The Validator confirms it’s not a false positive.
4. The Commander acts."

*(Action: Point to the Google Map)* 
"The map autonomously panned to the exact coordinates and rendered the crisis zone. The live scorecards at the top updated automatically parsing the agent traces, tracking exactly how many commuters were affected."

**Speaker 2:** "We didn't just build a wrapper. We built a highly modular, scalable, fault-tolerant orchestration engine. If our traffic APIs fail, CIRO autonomously drops into a secure mock loop to ensure the pipeline never crashes.
