# CIRO — Voiceover Script for AI-Narrated Demo

> **Purpose:** Text for AI text-to-speech narration (Google AI Studio, ElevenLabs, or similar)

> **Pacing:** Moderate speed with natural pauses at paragraph breaks

## Full Voiceover Text

Karachi. Sixteen million people. Every monsoon season, the city faces catastrophic urban flooding. Social media explodes with desperate cries for help — often in Roman Urdu. Emergency responders are overwhelmed with conflicting information. What if artificial intelligence could cut through the noise, validate signals against physical sensors, and coordinate a response — all in under six seconds?

[PAUSE 2 seconds]

This is CIRO — the Crisis Intelligence and Response Orchestrator. A production-oriented prototype built for AISeekho 2026, Challenge 3.

[PAUSE 1 second]

CIRO runs four specialized AI agents in sequence. The Sifter Agent ingests raw social media posts in Roman Urdu and fuses them with weather and traffic telemetry. The Strategist Agent cross-validates the incident, estimates the affected population, and generates rerouting plans. The Validator Gate — a deterministic safety layer — checks whether the social signals actually match the physical data. And the Commander Agent generates targeted citizen alerts and simulates dispatching emergency resources.

[PAUSE 2 seconds]

Watch what happens when we simulate a confirmed flood on University Road. The social posts report severe flooding. The weather API confirms forty-five millimeters of rainfall. Traffic congestion is at level eight out of ten. The Sifter detects four flood signals and assigns a severity of eight. The Strategist estimates forty-five thousand people at risk. The Validator confirms — strong social signals match strong telemetry. And the Commander dispatches five suction pumps, issues an SMS advisory, and creates a two-kilometer geofence around the incident zone.

[PAUSE 2 seconds]

But what happens when someone posts fake flood reports? Let's test it. The social signals claim severe flooding, but the weather API reports zero millimeters of rain. The Validator Gate catches this contradiction immediately — it flags the alert for operator review. No false dispatch. No wasted resources. This is AI safety in action.

[PAUSE 1 second]

And when the APIs themselves go down? CIRO doesn't crash. It activates Mock Fallback Mode, uses cached telemetry data, and clearly labels all results as simulated. The system degrades gracefully — it never goes blind.

[PAUSE 2 seconds]

CIRO was built with Google Antigravity as the principal development orchestrator. Antigravity designed the system architecture, generated the multi-agent pipeline, implemented the safety gates, built the mobile-first dashboard, and produced over twenty evidence files. One hundred and ten out of one hundred and eleven development tasks were completed under its direct orchestration.

[PAUSE 1 second]

The result? A working crisis command center that processes signals in under six seconds, catches false alarms before they waste resources, and costs zero dollars to operate on Firebase Spark and the Gemini free tier.

[PAUSE 2 seconds]

CIRO. Crisis intelligence that knows when to act — and when not to.

## AI Voice Generation Instructions

1. Copy the text above (without the [PAUSE] markers)
2. Open Google AI Studio, ElevenLabs, or your preferred TTS tool
3. Select a professional male or female voice
4. Set speaking rate to approximately 0.9x (slightly slower than default)
5. Insert natural pauses at paragraph breaks
6. Export as WAV or MP3
7. Import into video editor and sync with screen recording

- Total spoken words: ~380
- At ~140 words/minute: approximately **2 minutes 45 seconds**
